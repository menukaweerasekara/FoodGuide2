chinese gallery

dumpling

dumpling, small mass of leavened dough that is either boiled or steamed and served in soups.

Roasted Duck

Kung Pao Chicken

Versions commonly found in the West, called Kung Pao chicken, Kung Po, or just chicken chili and garlic, consist of diced, marinated chicken,

Sweet and Sour Pork

Sweet and sour pork is deep-fried pork, stir-fried with sweet pineapples and vibrant bell peppers covered in a bright sauce.

Dim Sum

Dim sum is a traditional Chinese meal made up of small plates of dumplings and other snack dishes and is usually accompanied by tea

Ma Po Tofu

It consists of tofu set in a spicy sauce, typically a thin, oily, and bright red suspension, based on douban

Char Siu

Char siu literally means "fork roasted" (siu being burn/roast and cha being fork, both noun and verb) after the traditional cooking method for the dish

Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Sichuan Pork

Sichuan cuisine, as we all know is famous for its hot and numbing dishes. Usage of spices is common in Sichuan dishes which gives some of their dishes a distinctive complex flavor.

Thai gallery

guay teow

Guay Tiew, (Thai: ก๋วยเตี๋ยว) meaning noodle, was adapted from Chinese cuisine by Thais who created their own style of noodles, soups and other elements like meat balls, dumplings, slow-cooked beef, roast pork, crispy egg pastry and the like to perfect their own market point.

Tom Yum Goong

A sour-flavoured spicy shrimp soup, tom yum kung's name comes from three Thai words. “Tom” means boiled, “yum” is a sour and spicy Thai salad and “kung” means prawn.

Tom Kha Gai

What Does Tom Yum Soup Taste Like? The flavor of tom yum should be sour upfront from lime juice, then salty from fish sauce, with a mild sweetness from nam prik pao paste.

Som Tam

A delicious Thai salad, Som Tam is a green papaya salad recipe that combines all four tastes - sour, chilli, sweet and salty. A healthy and delicious treat for brunch, lunch or a casual light dinner.

Yam Talay

Yum means mixture of different flavors, often associated with a salad, talay means seafood. This mixed seafood salad, Thai-style, is a winner.

Laab

Larb (Lao: ລາບ; Thai: ลาบ, RTGS: lap, pronounced [lâːp], also spelled laap, larp, lahb or laab) is a type of Lao meat salad that is the national dish of Laos, along with green papaya salad and sticky rice.

Pad Phuk Tong

What is PAD PHUK Tong? Fuk-Tong Pad Khai is a simple and easy Thai dish yet incredibly tasty. It is great eaten with turmeric rice and can be eaten as a main meal with the rice or also as a side dish with flat bread or chapattis.

Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a Thai dish of stir-fried rice noodles with eggs, vegetables and tofu in a sauce of tamarind, fish, dried shrimp, garlic, red chilli pepper and sugar.

Kai Jeow

Kai jeow (also spelled as khai jiao, khai jiaw, kai jeaw or kai jiew) is a traditional Thai fried omelet. What makes it unique is its puffed-up texture which is created by frying whisked eggs in hot oil.

Indian gallery

Aloo gobi

Aloo gobi (pronounced [aːlu ɡɔːbʱi]) is a vegetarian dish from the Indian subcontinent made with potatoes (aloo), cauliflower (gob(h)i), and Indian spices.

Butter chicken

Butter chicken, traditionally known as Murgh makhani, (pronounced [mʊrg ˈmək. kʰə.ni]) is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce.

Chana masala

Chana masala can also be called chole masala, referring to the kind of garam used in the dish. So if someday you see chole masala on a menu, then rest assured that it is your beloved chana masala

Palak paneer

It is a non-aged, non-melting soft cheese made by curdling milk with a fruit- or vegetable-derived acid, such as lemon juice.

Chicken tikka masala

Chicken tikka masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chicken chunks (chicken tikka) in spiced curry sauce.

Lamb Mutton

Mutton curry (also referred to as kosha mangsho, lamb curry, or goat curry) is a dish that is prepared from goat meat (or sometimes lamb meat) and vegetables.

Fish curry

Fish head curry is a Singaporean dish deriving from a hybrid of Indian and Chinese ethnic cuisines.

Lamb vindaloo

A highly seasoned dish of Indian origin made from meat or seafood cooked in a sauce of vinegar, red chilis, garlic, tamarind, and spices.

Dal makhani

Dal makhani (pronounced [daːl ˈmək. kʰə.ni]) is a dish originating in New Delhi, India. It is a modern take on the age-old urad ki dal (black lentil dal also known as maa ki dal).
